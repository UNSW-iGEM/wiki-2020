WEEK 1

31st August - 6th September

Cloning

  • Prepared LB broth and LB agar plates.
  • Assembled HSP22E and HSP22F inserts into pET-19b backbone with Gibson Assembly.
  • Transformed assembled plasmid into competent E. coli BL21 (DE3).
  • Colony PCR of transformed colonies. PCR product run on agarose gel electrophoresis.
  • Prepared starter cultures and performed QIAGEN Miniprep to extract and purify plasmids.
  • Purified plasmid sample sent for Sanger Sequencing.

WEEK 2

7th September - 13th September

Protein Expression and Purification

  • Prepared starter cultures of transformed colonies for induction.
  • Took patch plates and made glycerol stocks
  • Inoculated larger cultures and induced with IPTG.
  • Harvested and froze cells.
  • Conducted BugBuster protein expression check and SDS-PAGE to visualise results.
  • Prepared binding buffer and elution buffer stocks.
